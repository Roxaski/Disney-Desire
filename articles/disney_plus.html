Coming soon to Disney Plus

author : Blake Anderson

published : 22nd September

Let's take a look at some of the most popular shows coming to Disney Plus this September. We have seen so many great shows and movies being added to the service these last few months, so what can we expect this month.

Amongst some of the most popular recent additions is the Ahsoka series, set in the Star Wars universe and starting next month we will finally experience the epic conclusion to this phenomenal story, here's hoping that Disney decides to renew the show for another season, but if not we can at least forward to another series set in the Star Wars universe in the form of The Acolyte, we hope your as excited as we are for this new series!

  • i am groot | season 2
  • elemental
  • the barn dance

Kicking off our September list is the critically aclaimed The Little Mermaid, which is being added to the service after releasing at box office 4 months prior, we highly recommend this epic retelling of this incredible Disney classic.

Another wonderful film being added is the love story that will send sparks flying, Elemental is Pixar's latest blockbuster hit, raking in just shy of $500 million dollars at the box office. Now if you were a fan of the Marvel films, you won't want to miss out on this. Revisiting the most incredible and heartfelt scenes across many of Marvel's incredible stories.

Last but not least, we have a variety of series ranging from the ever cuter 'I am Groot' to the awesome cartoon 'The Ghost & Molly McGee', and more in between. Unfortunately, Molly McGee's second season will be it's last. Thankfully the creators 'Bill Motz and Bob Roth' who are behind this incredible cartoon series, have been given the greenlight to wrap the series up with one final episode.
